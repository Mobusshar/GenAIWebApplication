Emotion Bias(Creativity Inhibition) Potential Creativity Potential
Frustration 😤 May cause pessimistic thinking or resistance to new perspectives. Encourages problem-solving and new ideas due to discomfort.
Sadness 😢 Can lead to self-victimization and emotional exaggeration. Inspires deep reflection, art, and poetry.
Fear 😨 Often irrational and exaggerated, leading to paranoia. Triggers survival instincts, heightening imagination.
Anger 😡 Creates impulsive reactions and blame-shifting. Fuels passionate expression in literature and activism.
Empathy 🧡 Can be selective or manipulated based on personal biases. Encourages storytelling and character depth.
Gratitude 🙏 Sometimes romanticizes reality, ignoring struggles. Strengthens positive reinforcement in narratives.
Protectiveness 🛡️ May justify overprotective or exclusionary attitudes. Drives heroic themes in creative works.
Serenity 🌿 Can dismiss real conflicts in favor of forced optimism. Enhances peaceful, introspective creative works.
Joy 😄 Can overlook negative realities and suppress struggles. Motivates humor, satire, and uplifting stories.
Hope ✨ Can lead to unrealistic expectations. Inspires visions of progress and idealism.
Friendship 🤝 Can create in-group favoritism or bias. Strengthens themes of connection and collaboration.
Relief 😌 Can downplay the severity of issues. Encourages resolution and closure in storytelling.
Compassion 💞 May idealize emotions while neglecting complexities. Drives humanitarian creativity in writing and film.
Self-Reflection 🤔 Can become overly self-focused, losing objectivity. Leads to philosophical and introspective works.
Inspiration 🌟 Can be influenced by social biases or personal agendas. Encourages visionary thinking and artistic works.
